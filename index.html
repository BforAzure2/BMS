Features

Solving Healthcare Problem with Artificial
Intelligence and Cloud Computing

Patient Diagnostics and Care Module (PDCM)

This module is developed using state of the art (SOTA) AI based technologies and consist of a disparate array of AI based models for patient condition assessment and respective care required.

Learn More

Top k Disease Diagnose
(ICD-9 Codes)

This feature is responsible for creating a diagnosis of a patient based on the admission notes and caregiver notes.

Learn More

Patient Care Procedures Prediction (CPT Codes)

Similar to diagnosis prediction, this is an extreme multi-label task. In total, this module can predict 711 procedure codes in Current Procedural Terminology (CPT)

Learn More

Image Based Lab Analytics (CXR)

Image based labs including chest x-ray, CT scan and MRI plays a pivotal role for developing an informed diagnosis of the patients

Learn More

EEG/ECG Anomaly Detection (BP, Atrial Fibrilation etc.)

EEG/ECG anomaly detection can serve as a precursor to detailed diagnosis process for detection of diseases in Heart and Brain to gauge the health of the patients.

Learn More

Health Condition Severity

This module aims to provide a mortality prediction method for patients with severe disease including Sepsis, ARDS, Acute Kidney Infection based on machine learning using the EHR and other unstructured sensor information

Learn More

Physician Action prediction (from Discharge Notes)

Continuity of care is crucial to ensuring positive health outcomes for patients discharged from an inpatient hospital setting, and improved information sharing can help.

Learn More

Question Answering Module for Virtual OPD (Chatbot)

Medical professionals often query over clinical notes in Electronic Medical Records (EMRs) to find information that can support their decision making

Learn More

Hospital Resource planning Module (HRPM)

The HRPM is composed of AI based features to help automate the operational task like medical billing, efficient management ICU facilities and patient ward loads.

Learn More

Length of ICU Stay prediction

Early prediction of undesired outcomes among newly hospitalized patients could improve patient triage and prompt conversations about patients' goals of care

Learn More

Medical Billing using healthcare Professional Notes

Medical Coding is the critical element of a revenue cycle and drives the communication between healthcare providers and insurers

Learn More

Patient Re-admission Prediction

Hospital readmission shortly after discharge threatens the quality of patient care and leads to increased medical care costs

Learn More
